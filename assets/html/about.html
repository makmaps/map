About this site

This is a space for learning! For tripping and stumbling through spatial database analysis and asking every question that comes to mind, regardless of how silly it may sound. For writing powerful SQL queries that help to quickly uncover answers in half the time it might take our ESRI software to open. Above all, this is a space to share mapping apps however loud our puny human brain screams to stop~ Welcome!

About Me

Hi, I'm Makenzie! I am a spatial data wizard, a web mapping extraordinaire, a sometimes project manager, and certified goofball who has married my knowledge of GIS, databases, client and server side programming to answer questions about the world around us. I am passionate about creating beautiful, interactive and easy to understand visualizations, stories and online mapping products. Relying as much as I can on open source technologies and spatial databases, I enjoy exploring trends and patterns, such as examining what land has changed over time, or modeling what if scenarios, such as how adding a new road to an existing network will effect traffic patterns and noise pollution.

I currently live in West Cape May, New Jersey with my fellow geographer boyfriend and our two cats. I enjoy being active- biking, hiking and paddling around Cape Island during the warmer months. Come winter, you can find me traveling north to the mountains in search of powder. I am an active member of my local Environmental Commission and do my best to live as sustainably as possible, building a strong community and making meaningful connections with whoever crosses my path.
