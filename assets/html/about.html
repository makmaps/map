About Me

Hi, I'm Makenzie! I am a spatial data analyst, a web mapper,and a sometimes project manager who has intertwined my knowledge of GIS, databases, client and server side programming to answer questions about the world around us. I am passionate about creating beautiful, interactive and easy to understand visualizations, stories and online mapping products. Relying as much as I can on open source technologies and spatial databases, I enjoy exploring trends and patterns, such as examining what land has changed over time, or modeling what if scenarios, such as how adding a new road to an existing network will effect traffic patterns and noise pollution.

I currently live in West Cape May, New Jersey with my partner and our two cats. I enjoy being active- biking, hiking and paddling around Cape Island during the warmer months. Come winter, you can find me traveling north to the mountains in search of snow. I am an active member of my local Environmental Commission and do my best to live as sustainably as possible, building a strong community and making meaningful connections with whoever crosses my path.
