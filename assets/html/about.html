About Me

Hi, I'm Makenzie. I am a spatial data analyst, a web mapper and project manager who has intertwined my knowledge of GIS, databases, client and server side programming with my love of the natural and built world. I am passionate about creating beautiful, interactive and easy to understand visualizations, stories and online mapping products to help inform others. Relying as much as I can on open source technologies and spatial databases, I enjoy exploring trends and patterns, such as examining what land has changed over time, or modeling what if scenarios, such as how adding a new road to an existing network will effect traffic patterns and noise pollution.
